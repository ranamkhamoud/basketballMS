Help Section

Toolbar

  • Use the color picker to select the color of your drawings and shapes.
  • Click the "Add Text" button to add text to the whiteboard.
  • Click the "Clear" button to erase everything on the whiteboard.
  • Click the "Undo" button to undo your last drawing or shape.
  • Click the "Erase" button to activate the eraser tool.
  • Click the buttons under "Add Players" to add draggable player elements to the whiteboard.
  • Click the "Freehand" button to activate the freehand drawing tool.
  • Use the freehand width slider to adjust the width of your freehand drawings.
  • Click the "Save to Device" button to save an image of the whiteboard to your device.
  • Click the "Select" button to activate the select tool.
  • Click the "Add Outlined Circle" button to add an outlined circle shape to the whiteboard.
  • Click the "Add Outlined Triangle" button to add an outlined triangle shape to the whiteboard.
  • Click the "Add Rectangle" button to add a rectangle shape to the whiteboard.
